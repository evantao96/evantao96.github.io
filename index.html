About Me

I have been teaching AP Computer Science for 5 years at Central Park East High School, a high school in East Harlem, NYC.

Education is one of my interests, but I am mostly passionate about working with databases, networked systems and back-end infrastructure. I recently built a fully-functional music application using Ruby on Rails and the Spotify API. Right now I am working on a multiplayer Fantasy Olympics application in Node.js.

Aside from programming, I like to run. My next races are the Fred Lebow Half Marathon in NYC and the Seville Marathon in Spain.
